Welcome to the Neuroalgebra Project

About Neuroalgebra

Neuroalgebra is an interdisciplinary project that explores the intersection of neuroscience and algebraic structures. By applying algebraic concepts to neural systems, we aim to uncover hidden patterns and relationships within the complexity of the brain's structure and function.

Biochemistry Insights

The project delves into the intricate world of biochemistry, unraveling the molecular foundations of neural processes. From the elegant dance of neurotransmitters to the molecular machinery driving synaptic transmission, Neuroalgebra seeks to decipher the biochemical language of the brain.

Research Goals

Our research goals include:

  • Identifying algebraic structures in neural networks.
  • Understanding the biochemical basis of cognitive functions.
  • Exploring novel applications of algebra in neuroscience.
